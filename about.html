Who We Are

Bright Web Academy is focused on helping absolute beginners understand the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, and web structure in a simple, practical, and step-by-step manner.

Our Mission

We want every student to be able to:

  • Build clean and well-structured webpages
  • Understand semantic HTML and accessibility
  • Create small projects and share them with others

Journey So Far (Timeline)

2023 – Idea

The idea of a beginner-friendly HTML learning path was created.

2024 – Course Design

We designed a full HTML course with sections, chapters, and real projects.

2025 – Student Focus

We started helping students build complete websites and prepare for interviews.
